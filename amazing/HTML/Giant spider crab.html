Giant Spider Crab

It is considered one of the most terrifying and frightening creatures in the world, living with a length of its legs up to 4 meters and a weight that can reach 20 kg, enabling it to easily swallow a small child in addition to its claws can cause very great damage to humans up to death This spider is very difficult and rare to catch, as it lives in very large depths of the ocean, reaching at least 600 meters. The average spider is 100 years old and lives near the Japanese coast
