Mimic octopus (The King of disguise and the ocean wizard)

In the year one thousand nine hundred and 98 on the island of Sulawesi in Indonesia. Mimic octopus discovered The mimic octopus lives in shallow sandy depths near estuaries and is known for its abundance of predators. Most octopuses when exposed to attack follow a method that makes them less visible, such as disappearing between rocks or in pits, but the imitative octopus does not recognize the axioms, but rather follows the principle of attack is the best means of defense. It makes it look like the chosen animal and not only imitates its color or shape, but it also imitates the way it moves and imitates its personality, for example, in order to be able to move freely away from the depths, it disguises itself in the form of a lion fish known as the dragon fish. The mimic octopus changes the shape of its arms to appear like thorns after it changes its color to the color of the white fish in brown, and it mimics its swimming style.
